# Update Extension

{findBrowser()} updates your extensions automatically, but sometimes it can take a while for the extensions to be updated. Follow these simple steps to update your extensions manually:

1. Go to the <code>{findBrowser().toLowerCase()}://extensions</code> page and enable `Developer Mode`.
2. Click the `Update` button in the top-left corner.
3. All your installed extensions should now be updated.
