# Activate Extension

Follow these simple steps to activate your SuperDev Pro extension and unlock its full range of features:

## For New Install

1. Open the SuperDev Pro extension.
2. Enter your license key into the popup and click the `Activate` button.
3. You are all set! Start editing websites effortlessly with SuperDev Pro.

## For Pro Upgrade

1. Open the SuperDev Pro extension and click the settings ⚙️ icon.
2. Scroll to the bottom and click the `Click to Reset License` button.
3. Enter your license key into the popup and click the `Activate` button.
4. You are all set! Start editing websites effortlessly with SuperDev Pro.
